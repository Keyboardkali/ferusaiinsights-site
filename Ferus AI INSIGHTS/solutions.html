Research process

We use a consistent process so results are comparable and easy to use, while leaving room for the specifics of your question.

1. Define the brief

We clarify the decision, timeframe and constraints, and agree which markets, roles and workflows matter for this work.

2. Field and analyse

We combine surveys, interviews and our data collection models to gather the relevant signals, then segment, benchmark and stress-test the results.

3. Deliver & support

You receive focused decks and data cuts. Where helpful, we join internal discussions to help your team translate findings into action.
